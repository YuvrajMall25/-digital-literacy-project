\# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist

As a student and digital user, follow these important steps to protect yourself from cybercrime:

\## 🔐 General Safety Tips

\- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone.

\- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.

\- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) wherever possible.

\- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown emails.

\- Always verify the identity of unknown callers or messages.

\## 💸 UPI & Payment Safety

\- Remember: \*\*Scanning a QR code is used to PAY, not receive money.\*\*

\- Never approve unknown "collect requests" on UPI apps.

\- Double-check the receiver's name before sending money.

\- Do not trust strangers asking for advance payments or refunds.

\## 🌐 Social Media Safety

\- Keep your profiles private and limit personal information.

\- Avoid sharing sensitive details like phone number, address, or ID proofs.

\- Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.

\## ⚠️ Awareness & Responsibility

\- Stay updated about common cyber fraud techniques.

\- Report suspicious activity immediately.

\- Educate friends and family about digital safety.

\## 🚨 Report Cybercrime

\- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: <https://cybercrime.gov.in>

\- 📞 Helpline Number: 1930

👉 \*Be alert, stay informed, and use the internet responsibly.\*
